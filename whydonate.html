Why Donate Food ?

We need new Food donors from all the background to ensure there is right blood available for patients who needs it. We need:

  • • Atleast 40000 donors to meet the present demand
  • • Around 13500 donors a year to replace those who can no longer donate
  • • 3000 new donors with priority blood types such as O negative every year
  • • More young people to start giving blood so we can make sure we have   enough food in the future


# The most precious gift that one can give to another person is the gift of life i.e. food. It is the essence of life.

# Your food saves more than one life when it is separated into its components .

# Food is needed regularly for patients with diseases such as thalassemia and hemophilia, and also for the treatment of injuries after an accident, major surgeries, anemia, etc.

# It improves your mental health.
